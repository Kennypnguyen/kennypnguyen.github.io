The Rise of The Weeknd

A topic I can talk about is about the music R&B artist The Weeknd who has risen to fame and is now recognized globally. The Weeknd started off in the year 2011 releasing three mixtapes, which are now combined to be called Trilogy, and was quickly gaining traction because of the dark lyrics. Through the years, he has changed his style of music to more pop and new wave. Popular songs that include "The Hills", "Starboy", "Can't Feel My Face", "I Feel It Coming", and most notably "Blinding Lights". As of 2022, with his recent albums After Hours and Dawn FM, he is now considered one of the top artists of this decade. Although some of his older fans wish for him to go back to his dark lyrics, they are still huge supporters and fans of The Weeknd as he continues to gain more fans.
